# Apparel & Leather Technics ERP

## What is ALTechnics ERP?

ALTechnics ERP is a comprehensive Enterprise Resource Planning system built specifically for **Apparel & Leather Technics Pvt. Ltd.** It manages the complete business lifecycle from lead generation through sales, inventory management, invoicing, and after-sales service.

## Key Features

- **Customer Relationship Management** — Track customers, manage leads with kanban boards, convert leads to customers
- **Sales Pipeline** — Create quotations, convert to sales orders, generate invoices, track payments
- **Inventory Management** — Multi-warehouse stock tracking, stock movements, low stock alerts, goods receipts
- **Purchase Management** — Vendor management, purchase orders, partial goods receipts
- **Invoicing & Payments** — Professional PDF invoices, payment tracking, overdue management
- **Service Management** — Service ticket tracking, technician assignment, resolution workflows
- **Role-Based Access** — 7 roles with granular permission matrix
- **Dashboard & Reports** — Real-time analytics, sales trends, exportable reports
- **Public Documentation** — This very site!

## Who Is This For?

This ERP is designed for small to medium apparel and leather goods manufacturers/traders who need:
- End-to-end order-to-cash management
- Inventory tracking with multiple warehouses
- GST-compliant invoicing
- Role-based team access
