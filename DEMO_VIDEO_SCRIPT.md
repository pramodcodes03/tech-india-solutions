# 🎥 ERP Demo Video — Recording Script

**Target length:** 6–7 minutes
**Audience:** SME business owners, operations heads, decision-makers
**Goal:** Client understands the product in minutes and says *"Let's proceed."*

---

## 🎬 Pre-Recording Checklist

Before you hit record, set the stage. A clean screen is half the demo.

- [ ] Log in as **admin** with full permissions (so every module is visible)
- [ ] Seed **realistic demo data** — customer names like "Sharma Traders," amounts like ₹45,000, real product names (not "Test 1", "abc")
- [ ] Ensure dashboard shows: sales this month, 1–2 overdue invoices, a low-stock item, 1 open ticket (for visual impact)
- [ ] Close all browser tabs except the ERP
- [ ] Screen resolution: **1920×1080**, browser zoom **100%**
- [ ] Hide bookmarks bar, enable clean profile
- [ ] Use **Loom / OBS** with cursor highlighter ON
- [ ] Mic check — quiet room, no echo
- [ ] Keep a glass of water, speak calmly, **smile while recording** (it shows in voice)

---

## 🕐 SECTION 1 — Introduction (0:00 – 0:35)

**Duration:** 30–35 seconds
**Show on screen:** Login page → then blur/pause briefly on your logo

**Script (what to say):**

> "Hi, thank you for your time. In the next 6 minutes, I'll walk you through our ERP — a single platform built for growing businesses to manage **leads, sales, inventory, billing, and reports — all in one place.**
>
> Whether you're handling 10 orders a month or 1000, this system gives you complete visibility and control — without the complexity of traditional enterprise software.
>
> Let's jump in."

**On-screen actions:**
1. Show the login page (2 sec)
2. Enter credentials, login smoothly
3. Land on the dashboard

---

## 🕐 SECTION 2 — Dashboard Overview (0:35 – 1:30)

**Duration:** 55 seconds
**Show:** [Admin Dashboard](/admin/dashboard)

**Script:**

> "This is your **command center.** The moment you log in, you know exactly where your business stands.
>
> At the top, four live metrics:
> - **Sales this month** — ₹[amount], tracked in real-time
> - **Total receivables** — money owed to you
> - **Low stock alerts** — products running out
> - **Open service tickets** — customer issues needing attention
>
> Notice the **overdue invoice alert** here — one click, and you're on the collection call. No digging through spreadsheets."

**On-screen actions:**
1. Slowly hover over each of the 4 stat cards (1 sec each)
2. Scroll down to the overdue invoices widget
3. Click one overdue invoice → show invoice detail → click back

**🎯 Tip:** Zoom into the stat cards using your screen recorder's zoom feature.

---

## 🕐 SECTION 3 — Core Modules Walkthrough (1:30 – 5:00)

### 3A. Lead & CRM Management (1:30 – 2:15) — 45 sec

**Show:** [Leads Kanban Board](/admin/leads/kanban)

**Script:**

> "Every business runs on leads. Here's our **visual sales pipeline** — leads organized by stage: New, Contacted, Qualified, Proposal, Won.
>
> Your sales team simply **drags a lead forward** as it progresses. No status updates, no forms — just drag.
>
> When a lead converts, one click turns them into a customer. No re-entering data."

**On-screen actions:**
1. Show the Kanban board (full view)
2. **Drag one lead** from "Contacted" to "Qualified" — hold it slowly so viewers see the motion
3. Click a "Qualified" lead → show **Convert to Customer** button
4. Brief glimpse of the list view (optional, 2 sec)

**🌟 Wow moment:** The drag-drop animation. Do it SLOWLY.

---

### 3B. Sales & Quotation Flow (2:15 – 3:10) — 55 sec

**Show:** Quotations → Sales Orders

**Script:**

> "Now the sale. From a customer request, we create a **quotation in seconds** — add products, apply discounts, set terms.
>
> Customer approves? One click converts the quotation into a **sales order.** No re-typing, no errors.
>
> You can also download a professional PDF to share instantly."

**On-screen actions:**
1. Navigate to Quotations → open one existing quote
2. Show the line items, total, PDF download button
3. Click **Convert to Sales Order** → show the auto-created order
4. Briefly scroll through the sales order detail

---

### 3C. Inventory with Low-Stock Intelligence (3:10 – 3:55) — 45 sec

**Show:** [Inventory → Low Stock](/admin/inventory/low-stock)

**Script:**

> "Stock-outs cost sales. Our **Inventory module** tracks every product, every warehouse, in real-time.
>
> See this **red badge in the sidebar?** That's the system telling you — right now — which items are running low. Click it…
>
> …and here's the exact list. Reorder before you lose a sale."

**On-screen actions:**
1. Point cursor at the **Low Stock badge** in the sidebar (zoom in!)
2. Click it → show the low-stock report
3. Hover over one product, show reorder level vs. current stock
4. Briefly click "Current Stock" to show the overall inventory view

**🌟 Wow moment:** The sidebar badge. It's subtle but powerful.

---

### 3D. Invoicing & Payments (3:55 – 4:40) — 45 sec

**Show:** Invoices → Create → Payments

**Script:**

> "From the sales order, generating an invoice takes **one click.** Tax calculated, balance tracked automatically.
>
> Send the PDF to the customer, record their payment — full, partial, multiple modes — and the system tracks the balance due without you touching a calculator."

**On-screen actions:**
1. Open an existing invoice (show the professional layout)
2. Click **Download PDF** briefly
3. Click **Record Payment** → show the form
4. Submit → show status change to "Paid" or "Partial"

---

### 3E. Reports — The Decision Layer (4:40 – 5:15) — 35 sec

**Show:** [Reports Dashboard](/admin/reports)

**Script:**

> "And none of this matters without insight. Our **Reports module** gives you five key views — Sales, Inventory, Customers, Purchases, Payments.
>
> Filter by date, customer, or product — export to Excel or PDF for your accountant. That's your month-end in 30 seconds."

**On-screen actions:**
1. Show the reports landing page with 5 report cards
2. Click Sales Report → pick a date range → show the chart/table
3. Click Export to Excel (briefly)

---

## 🕐 SECTION 4 — End-to-End Real Business Flow (5:15 – 6:15)

**Duration:** 1 minute
**Show:** Sequential walkthrough — keep it tight

**Script:**

> "Let me show you how this all connects. A real business day:
>
> **Step 1 — Lead comes in.** Marked Qualified. Drag to Proposal.
> **Step 2 — Convert to customer.** One click.
> **Step 3 — Send a quotation.** Customer approves.
> **Step 4 — Quotation becomes a sales order.** Inventory reserves stock automatically.
> **Step 5 — Invoice generated. Payment recorded.**
>
> Five steps. One platform. Zero duplicate data entry.
>
> That's a lead-to-cash cycle your team can run in minutes, not days."

**On-screen actions:**
- Move quickly through each screen — **2 seconds per screen max**
- Don't create new data; use pre-seeded demo data and just click through
- End on the paid invoice showing "PAID" status

---

## 🕐 SECTION 5 — Key Benefits (6:15 – 6:50)

**Duration:** 35 seconds
**Show:** Dashboard (back to start) with 3 on-screen text callouts if editing

**Script:**

> "So what does this mean for your business?
>
> ✅ **Saves hours every week** — no spreadsheets, no duplicate entry
> ✅ **One central system** — your team, your data, one place
> ✅ **Complete visibility** — from first lead to final payment

**On-screen actions:**
1. Return to the dashboard
2. Optionally visit **Settings → Roles** briefly to show the permission matrix (3 seconds)

>
> We handle the setup, training, and data migration for you. Our team is available for support whenever you need us.

**On-screen actions:**

2. Hold the closing slide for 3 seconds before stopping recording

## 🎬 POST-RECORDING CHECKLIST

- [ ] Trim dead air at start and end
- [ ] Add light background music (royalty-free, low volume ~10%)
- [ ] Add **captions/subtitles** — many buyers watch muted
- [ ] Add zoom-ins on small text (stat cards, badges)
- [ ] Add smooth transitions between sections (not flashy — keep it corporate)
- [ ] Keep total length **under 7 minutes** — cut ruthlessly if over
- [ ] Export at **1080p**, share as Loom link or MP4

---

## 🗣️ VOICE & DELIVERY TIPS

| Do | Don't |
|---|---|
| Speak at 85% of your normal pace | Rush through features |
| Pause 1 sec between sections | Narrate every click |
| Use "you" and "your business" | Use "the user" or "one can" |
| Say numbers confidently (₹45,000) | Say "umm," "like," "basically" |
| Sound like a trusted advisor | Sound like a feature list |

## 🎯 THE ONE THING TO REMEMBER

> Clients don't buy features. They buy **outcomes.**
> Every time you show a screen, connect it back to a result: *time saved, mistakes avoided, sales captured, visibility gained.*

Good luck. Record it in **one clean take** after 2–3 practice runs. That's when it sounds natural.
